The Opportunity

Birthdays are moments to celebrate individual milestones and create lasting memories with loved ones. At Events by Kariuki, we specialize in transforming birthday celebrations into unforgettable experiences that honor the guest of honor while delighting all attendees.

From milestone birthdays (16, 21, 30, 50) to intimate family gatherings, we've orchestrated over 2,000 celebrations. Each event is crafted with attention to personal preferences, thematic concepts, and the unique personality of the celebrant. We believe every birthday deserves that special touch.

Whether you're envisioning an elegant soirée, a vibrant costume ball, or an adventurous outdoor celebration, our team brings creative vision and flawless execution to make your day truly memorable.

The Idea

Our birthday design philosophy centers on personalization and celebration. We collaborate closely with clients to capture their vision—whether that's a vintage glamour theme, a modern minimalist aesthetic, or a whimsical fantasy concept. Every detail, from invitations to décor to entertainment, reflects the essence of the celebration.

We integrate interactive elements that engage guests—photo booths with custom backdrops, live entertainment, surprise performances, and immersive experiences. Sustainable practices ensure our celebrations are thoughtful, using eco-friendly materials and minimizing environmental impact while maximizing joy.

The Execution

Executing a perfect birthday celebration requires careful planning and adaptable coordination. Our timeline spans weeks before the event: concept development, vendor coordination with caterers and entertainers, décor fabrication, and logistics planning.

We conduct regular check-ins with the client, manage all vendor relationships, and maintain detailed timelines for every element. On the day of the celebration, our team arrives early for setup, manages entertainment flow, coordinates surprises, and ensures every guest feels the warmth and joy of the occasion.
