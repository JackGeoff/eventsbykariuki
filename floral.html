The Opportunity

Flowers are the poetry of events. They soften spaces, add natural elegance, and create emotional resonance. At Events by Kariuki, our team of 60+ master florists designs and installs botanical arrangements that transform venues into gardens—whether we're creating intimate table florals or sweeping installation pieces.

We've arranged florals for 2800+ events, sourcing rare specimens, seasonal blooms, and exotic flowers from 100+ worldwide partnerships. Our approach balances aesthetic vision with horticultural expertise. We understand flower lifecycles, water requirements, and preservation techniques ensuring blooms remain vibrant throughout your event.

From classic rose arrangements to avant-garde installations, architectural floral statements to living plant installations, we design florals that complement your event's design while adding natural, organic beauty.

The Idea

Exceptional florals begin with consultation. We explore your aesthetic preferences, seasonal considerations, color palettes, and budget. Working with your event's design system, we craft floral schemes that feel cohesive yet distinctive—every arrangement intentional, every placement considered.

Our designs incorporate diverse elements: table centerpieces in varying heights, statement installation pieces, processional flowers, backdrop arrangements, and ceremony installations. We consider sightlines, dining functionality, and sensory impact—ensuring florals enhance rather than obstruct the experience.

The Execution

Floral execution requires precision timing and botanical expertise. Our team sources materials, conditions flowers, prepares arrangements in temperature-controlled workspaces, and delivers installations with expert on-site placement. We manage water systems, monitor bloom health, and make real-time adjustments throughout your event.

Large installations receive comprehensive installation plans, structural assessment, and on-site setup management. Our florists remain available throughout your event for any necessary maintenance—removing spent blooms, refreshing water, ensuring arrangements maintain their intended impact.
