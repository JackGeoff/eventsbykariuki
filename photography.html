The Opportunity

Photography is the art of preserving moments. At Events by Kariuki, our award-winning photographers capture the essence of your event—the joyful expressions, the intimate moments, the energy of the celebration. Every photograph tells a story.

With 10,000+ events documented, our team understands how to anticipate moments, manage challenging lighting conditions, and deliver images that are both technically flawless and emotionally resonant. Whether capturing wedding vows, corporate keynotes, or candid celebrations, we deliver compelling visual narratives.

Our photography service extends beyond event day—we offer comprehensive editing, album design, digital archiving, and custom print products that allow you to relive your event for years to come.

The Idea

Our photography philosophy balances technical excellence with artistic vision. We employ multiple photographers to ensure comprehensive coverage—capturing wide venue shots, intimate details, candid interactions, and formal portraits. Our approach combines photojournalistic documentation with styled group photography.

We leverage cutting-edge camera technology, drone capabilities for aerial perspectives, and advanced editing techniques to deliver images that exceed expectations. Every photograph is carefully curated and professionally processed to create a cohesive visual narrative of your event.

The Execution

Photographing major events requires positioning multiple photographers strategically throughout the venue, maintaining constant communication between team members, and adapting to changing light and action. Our photographers arrive early for venue assessment and spend the entire event capturing moments.

Post-event, our editing team carefully selects the best images, applies consistent color grading and retouching, and delivers a final gallery within weeks. We offer various delivery formats—digital galleries, printed albums, and custom books—ensuring you have lasting memories of your celebration.
