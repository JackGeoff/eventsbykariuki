The Opportunity

Modern events demand sophisticated audio-visual systems. From intimate gatherings to massive productions, sound quality and visual displays define guest experience. At Events by Kariuki, our 100-person AV team delivers broadcast-quality sound, stunning projection mapping, sophisticated lighting systems, and complex interactive displays for 3200+ events across 5M+ square meters of venues.

We source cutting-edge equipment—crystalline PA systems, multi-format projection capabilities, architectural lighting rigs, video wall systems, and immersive audio experiences. We design systems that serve function (clear sound, visible displays) while elevating aesthetics through sophisticated execution.

Our technical expertise spans all event requirements: live presentations, DJ systems, live band technical support, video content production, projection mapping, lighting design, and interactive installations.

The Idea

exceptional AV design balances technical capability with aesthetic integration. We conduct comprehensive venue assessments to understand acoustics, sightline challenges, electrical capacity, and environmental factors. We design systems that deliver technical excellence while remaining visually elegant.

Our approach integrates audio, video, and lighting cohesively—coordinating timing, color grading, sound levels, and dynamic effects to create immersive experiences. We develop custom content, coordinate real-time programming, and build systems responsive to event flow and entertainment requirements.

The Execution

Professional AV execution requires meticulous planning and real-time management. Our teams conduct comprehensive site surveys, design detailed system schematics, manage equipment logistics, install systems with precision, and execute comprehensive technical rehearsals. We verify every projection, test audio coverage, validate lighting cues, and ensure all interactive systems function flawlessly.

During your event, our head engineer manages all technical operations, monitors system performance, adjusts sound levels in real-time, coordinates lighting cues, manages video content playback, and handles any technical challenges. Our teams remain responsive throughout, ensuring technical excellence that supports your event's success.
