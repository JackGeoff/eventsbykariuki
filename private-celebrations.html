The Opportunity

Private celebrations demand discretion, elegance, and impeccable attention to detail. Whether hosting an anniversary gala, milestone celebration, or intimate reunion, Events by Kariuki specializes in creating unforgettable private events that feel personal and extraordinary.

Our approach focuses on understanding your vision and translating it into a curated experience. We've orchestrated intimate affairs for hundreds of valued clients, understanding that privacy, exclusivity, and personalization are paramount. Every element—from venue selection to guest experience—reflects the values and personality of those being celebrated.

The Idea

Our private event design philosophy emphasizes intimacy without sacrificing sophistication. We curate bespoke experiences that feel distinctly personal—whether through custom menu curation, curated entertainment, or tailored décor that tells a specific story.

Discretion is woven into every layer. We manage guest lists with confidentiality, coordinate with trusted vendor partners, and ensure privacy protocols are maintained throughout. Sustainability aligns with luxury—we source consciously, minimize waste, and create events that feel both lavish and responsible.

The Execution

Executing a private celebration requires discretion, precision, and flexibility. Our planning process involves confidential consultations, careful vendor vetting, and detailed coordination to ensure every element aligns with your vision.

On the day of the event, our boutique team works invisibly—managing logistics while allowing you and your guests to simply enjoy the celebration. We maintain strict privacy protocols and adapt seamlessly to any requests that enhance the experience.
