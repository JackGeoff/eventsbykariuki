The Opportunity

Video is the most powerful medium for reliving your event. At Events by Kariuki, our cinematographers capture the narrative arc of your celebration—the anticipation, the peak moments, the emotional resonance. Every frame is crafted to evoke feeling.

With 2000+ events filmed and countless awards for our work, we understand how to translate live events into compelling cinematic experiences. Our approaches range from documentary-style footage to highly stylized production, always delivered with broadcast-quality standards.

Beyond event day, we offer comprehensive post-production—from 4K color grading to motion graphics to sound design—creating highlight reels, full event documentation, and custom deliverables that serve multiple purposes.

The Idea

Our videography philosophy emphasizes storytelling and emotional impact. We deploy multiple camera operators with cinema-grade equipment, capturing wide establishing shots, intimate details, and dynamic action. Our approach balances capturing authentic moments with intentional cinematography.

We incorporate drone footage for sweeping venue views, gimbal-stabilized motion for immersive sequences, and strategic coverage that creates dramatic visual variety. Every shot is framed with an eye toward the final edit, ensuring seamless narrative flow.

The Execution

Filming comprehensive event coverage requires strategic positioning, real-time communication between operators, and technical precision. Our videography teams arrive hours before your event, conduct comprehensive lighting assessment, and position themselves at critical junctures to capture the essential narrative.

Post-production spans weeks of editing, color correction, sound design, and effects integration. We deliver a main highlight reel (5-10 minutes), full event documentation, ceremony/key moments compilation, and custom clips optimized for social media sharing.
