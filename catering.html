The Opportunity

Food is memory. The cuisine served at your event becomes part of the story—referenced, remembered, celebrated for years. At Events by Kariuki, we don't simply cater; we orchestrate elevated culinary experiences that reflect your event's essence and your guests' expectations.

Our network of 80+ award-winning chefs brings expertise spanning global cuisines, fine dining techniques, and innovative interpretations. We've catered over 3000 events, from intimate dinners of 20 to galas of 5000+, each with bespoke menu curation, seasonal sourcing, and dietary precision.

Beyond the meal itself, we design the entire dining experience—table presentations, service choreography, wine pairings, and culinary theater moments that elevate your event from dinner to dining experience.

The Idea

Our culinary philosophy begins with consultation. We understand your event's theme, your guests' preferences, dietary requirements, and your budget. Working with your vision, our chefs design progressive menus that tell a story through courses—each dish intentional, each flavor profile considered.

We source premium ingredients, many from exclusive suppliers, sustainable farms, and artisanal producers. Every menu is designed for proper execution timing, ensuring hot courses arrive hot and plated dishes are pristine. We incorporate interactive elements—chef's stations, tableside preparations, culinary theater—that transform dinner into experience.

The Execution

Executing flawless catering for large-scale events requires military precision. Our culinary teams conduct full kitchen setup, implement HVAC and timing protocols for optimal food quality, and coordinate a sophisticated service ballet that appears effortless. We manage multiple stations, dietary accommodations, and real-time adjustments seamlessly.

Our service staff receive comprehensive training specific to your event's culture and choreography. We provide wine service, bar operations, and specialized offerings—from welcome cocktails to late-night dessert stations to midnight snacks. Every guest receives personalized attention and exceptional cuisine.
