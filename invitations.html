The Opportunity

The invitation is the first message guests receive. It establishes tone, communicates essential information, and builds anticipation. At Events by Kariuki, we've designed 5M+ invitations—from traditional printed formats to sophisticated digital experiences—each reflecting your event's brand and aesthetic.

Our team of 75 specialists includes designers, copywriters, production managers, and technology experts. We create invitations that align perfectly with your event design system while optimizing information architecture, clarity, and guest response rates. We achieve 98% RSVP rates through expertly crafted messaging and intuitive response mechanisms.

Beyond initial invitations, we manage comprehensive guest communication: reminder emails, welcome sequences, last-minute updates, and post-event follow-ups. Every message reinforces your event's prestige and manages expectations flawlessly.

The Idea

Exceptional invitations balance aesthetics with functionality. We work with your event's design system to develop invitation concepts—color palette, typography, special effects like embossing, foiling, or die-cutting. Every design element supports your event's positioning and brand identity.

Our copywriting emphasizes clarity and tone-setting. We craft messaging that communicates event details while building excitement and establishing appropriate formality or playfulness. For digital invitations, we design interactive experiences—animated reveals, embedded videos, customized gifting links—that increase engagement and drive attendance.

The Execution

Production quality defines the guest experience. We source premium papers, employ specialty printing techniques, manage complex die-cutting and assembly, and ensure every invitation reflects your event's luxury positioning. Our quality control process verifies color accuracy, printing clarity, and flawless assembly.

Distribution coordination ensures invitations arrive at optimal timing—early enough for calendar holds, close enough to maintain anticipation. We manage digital delivery, provide backup production capacity for rush situations, and maintain comprehensive guest management systems tracking responses, dietary requirements, and special accommodations.
