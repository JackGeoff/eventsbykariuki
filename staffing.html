The Opportunity

Your event's success depends fundamentally on your team. The right staff—trained, professional, empowered—elevates guest experience and ensures flawless operations. At Events by Kariuki, we staff 4500+ events annually with 2000+ team members: coordinators, concierge, service personnel, technical crew, managers, and specialists.

Our staffing model emphasizes training and professionalism. Every team member receives comprehensive event briefing, understands your brand positioning, and knows their role within the larger operation. We employ professionals with hospitality background, cultural sensitivity, and exceptional customer service instincts.

Staffing coordination spans planning (team structure, roles, coverage), recruiting qualified professionals, conducting thorough training, managing logistics, and overseeing operations. Our leadership ensures your event runs smoothly with minimal guest-facing stress.

The Idea

Exceptional staffing requires careful planning. We develop team structures matched to your event's requirements—greeters for initial guest engagement, concierge for assistance, coat check personnel, restroom attendants, bar staff, service teams, runner functions, and management oversight. Each role supports guest experience and operational efficiency.

We recruit professionals from our network, conduct comprehensive vetting, and provide detailed training specific to your event. Staff uniforms, behavior standards, and service protocols are clearly established. We emphasize proactive guest service—staff anticipate needs, provide assistance gracefully, and handle inevitable challenges with professionalism and discretion.

The Execution

Professional team execution requires detailed management. Our coordinators conduct pre-event briefings, verify uniform fit and appearance, distribute role assignments, and establish communication protocols. Teams arrive early, complete setup assignments, position themselves at key locations, and remain responsive throughout your event.

A head manager oversees all operations, communicates with staff, monitors guest experience, addresses issues rapidly, and adapts team positioning in response to real-time needs. Staff maintain professionalism under pressure, handle unexpected situations with grace, and consistently exceed guest expectations through exceptional service.
