The Opportunity

Events are logistical challenges disguised as celebrations. Coordinating vendors, managing timelines, controlling costs, and ensuring flawless execution requires sophisticated project management. At Events by Kariuki, our 200+ logistics professionals have coordinated 5000+ events, maintaining 99.9% timeline compliance.

Our approach treats events as complex operational projects requiring meticulous planning, vendor coordination, contingency management, and real-time problem-solving. We integrate all event components—design, catering, entertainment, technical, decorative, staffing—into coherent execution plans that deliver results on time and within budget.

Logistics excellence means you experience your event in real-time while our team manages complexity invisibly. Vendor coordination happens behind scenes, timeline pressures are resolved before they impact guests, and creative vision translates seamlessly into reality.

The Idea

Exceptional logistics requires comprehensive planning. We develop master project schedules identifying all activities, dependencies, critical path items, and contingency buffers. Vendor coordination spans contract negotiation, timeline confirmation, delivery logistics, setup verification, and quality assurance.

We maintain detailed event day playbooks documenting every timeline item, vendor contact information, emergency protocols, and communication networks. Digital dashboards provide real-time visibility into logistics status. Regular coordination meetings ensure all teams understand their roles and work cohesively toward shared success.

The Execution

Event day logistics requires disciplined execution against detailed plans. Our project managers arrive early, verify vendor setup, confirm timeline compliance, manage real-time adjustments, and maintain communication with all parties. We track critical items, identify issues before they escalate, and implement solutions rapidly.

Multiple coordinators manage different functions simultaneously while remaining in constant communication through headsets and digital systems. Our heads of logistics maintain high-level oversight, empower teams to solve problems, and escalate only true exceptions. Guest experience remains smooth while logistics complexity is managed seamlessly behind the scenes.
