The Opportunity

The planning stage defines event success. The right counsel—early, strategic, experienced—guides you through complexity toward events of genuine impact. At Events by Kariuki, our 85 expert consultants have guided 6500+ events through conception, planning, and execution, with 98% client satisfaction.

Our consultants bring diverse expertise: event strategists, creative directors, operational experts, budget specialists, and industry specialists. Whether you're hosting your first event or your hundredth, our guidance translates your vision into actionable plans while managing budget, logistics, and expectations.

Consultation spans strategic planning (event purpose, target experience, success metrics), creative direction (concept development, design strategy), operational planning (timeline development, vendor identification, budget management), and implementation support (vendor coordination, quality assurance, real-time problem-solving).

The Idea

Exceptional consultation begins with deep listening. We conduct thorough discovery conversations: What's your vision? What outcome are you seeking? What's your budget? What constraints exist? We ask questions that clarify thinking and identify opportunities. From this foundation, we develop comprehensive event strategies.

Our consultants provide candid counsel: recommending concepts that serve your purpose, steering away from approaches that won't work, identifying budget optimization opportunities, and suggesting vendor partners or services you might not have considered. We balance aspiration with realism, supporting ambitious vision while ensuring achievability.

The Execution

Consultation extends beyond planning into implementation support. Our consultants connect you with vetted vendors, facilitate introductions, advise on negotiations, and provide ongoing quality oversight. We remain available throughout your event planning process for guidance, troubleshooting, and course correction.

We provide tools: detailed planning checklists, timeline templates, vendor evaluation frameworks, budget trackers, and communication templates. Our consultants are accessible for questions, provide real-time problem-solving support, and ensure you feel confident throughout your journey from concept to completion.
