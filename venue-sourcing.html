The Opportunity

Venue selection defines event success. The right space—with proper capacity, aesthetic appeal, technical capability, and logistical support—sets the stage for everything else. At Events by Kariuki, we maintain relationships with 4000+ venues spanning luxury hotels, exclusive estates, museums, industrial spaces, outdoor locations, and custom installations.

Our 90 venue specialists understand each location's capabilities, limitations, and negotiation dynamics. We assess venues against your requirements, secure optimal pricing, negotiate favorable terms, and manage all logistics—parking, loading, technical infrastructure, compliance, and contingency planning.

Whether you need an iconic location that provides instant prestige or a blank canvas for custom design, we find the perfect venue and manage all associated logistics seamlessly.

The Idea

Venue sourcing requires understanding your objectives, aesthetic preferences, budget parameters, and operational requirements. We explore current inventory, assess emerging venues, and present options with comprehensive analysis—site photos, capacity details, technical specifications, pricing, and logistical considerations.

We manage venue negotiations directly with management, securing optimal terms and ensuring favorable contract language. We conduct detailed site assessments for your chosen venue, verify all technical and logistical capabilities, identify potential challenges, and develop comprehensive venue operation plans.

The Execution

Venue logistics management requires comprehensive planning and real-time coordination. We conduct detailed walkthroughs with venue management, establish clear operational protocols, coordinate vendor access, manage parking and loading logistics, verify technical infrastructure, and ensure compliance with all venue requirements.

Our on-site venue coordinators manage all venue-related operations throughout your event: vendor coordination, guest flow management, parking management, technical support, and rapid response to any issues. We maintain constant communication with venue management, ensuring seamless operations and guest comfort.
