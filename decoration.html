The Opportunity

Decoration transforms ordinary spaces into extraordinary environments. Our team of 150 decoration experts has beautified 3800+ events, creating cohesive, visually stunning spaces that amplify your event's design intention. From intimate table styling to large-scale venue transformation, we handle all decorative elements with precision.

We maintain expansive inventory: linens in custom colors, table settings, centerpiece frameworks, uplighting systems, backdrop installations, and specialty piece libraries. Working with your design system, we source or create decorative elements that reflect your aesthetic while staying within budget parameters.

Decoration excellence means attention to detail across every surface: tables, walls, architectural features, entry sequences, restroom spaces. Your guests experience cohesive beauty at every location they visit, reinforcing your event's professionalism and prestige.

The Idea

Exceptional decoration begins with comprehensive design planning. We develop detailed decoration specifications—table styles, centerpiece sketches, linen selections, color coordination, special architectural treatments. Every element connects to your overall design vision, creating visual harmony throughout the venue.

We incorporate both purchased items and custom creations—commission specialty pieces, create elaborate backdrops, design interactive installations. Our approach balances stunning aesthetics with practical functionality, ensuring decorations support rather than obstruct guest experiences.

The Execution

Professional decorators manage precise timing and orchestrated setup. Our teams arrive early, establish efficient layout plans, and execute decoration installation with coordinated efficiency. We manage multiple stations simultaneously—table setup, wall installations, accent lighting placement, special element positioning.

Quality control verifies consistency across all tables, validates color accuracy, ensures structural stability of installations, and performs final adjustments. Our decoration teams remain on-site throughout your event, managing breakage, refreshing arrangements, and making any necessary adjustments to maintain visual excellence.
